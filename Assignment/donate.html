    Get Involved – Become a Volunteer

    Make a difference with the young people in our community by offering your time.

    What would you like to do?

    Donate – donate or sponsor some of our equipment. Every year we help hundreds of young people to take part in sport. With your generosity we can continue this vital work – there are lots of way you can donate:

    • Make a one-off donation
      Regular giving - make a monthly donation

    • Fundraise – because we are a charity we could not do the work we do without support. Some ideas for fundraising on our behalf are:

    • Take part in a challenge event and have friends sponsor you
      Organise a pub quiz night at your favourite pub
      Host a cake sale or coffee morning
      Have a dressing up day at work

    • Remember we always want to hear about the work our fundraisers are doing, please get in touch and let us know what you are up to. We always love adding pictures and stories to our website.

    Volunteer - in sport sessions and coaching opportunities here at SportingKids we run a number of events throughout the year. Maybe you would like to spend a Saturday at the BMX track assisting with a club session or perhaps you would like to gain some event planning experience and work with our fundraising team. We would love to have you on board.
